Day in Port Hope

⏱ 2h · Walk · Streets

Streets, Locks

Port Hope is a comfy, riverfront town not far from Cobourg.

Pictoresque downtown has been featured in number of film producions.

Historical Capitol Theatre, operating with some interruptions since 1930-s.

Streets are flowing around Ganaraska river, where you can see salmon jumping the ladder to get past Corbett's Dam.

Rotary Centennial Riveriew Park right under Hwy 401.

Port Hope Harbour

Port Hope also houses uranium conversion facility of Cameco Corporation, located across Port Hope Harbour.
